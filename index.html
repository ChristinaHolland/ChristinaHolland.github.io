About me

I am a tenacious problem solver and data scientist in search of new ways to use data to improve the world around me. With a PhD in physical oceanography, I have a deep background in modeling and large databases. As a former high school mathematics teacher I am able to convey complex concepts and results to both technical and non technical audiences.

When I'm not exploring the world of data through my computer, I like to explore the world right around me. I enjoy gardening, hiking and geocaching with my family.
